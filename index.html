Who We Are

We don't coach.
We architect careers.

The Executive Edge is a global career strategy firm built for ambitious managers and high-potentials who refuse to leave their trajectory to chance. With over 20 years of direct engagement with C-Level executives, board directors and talent valuation leaders across Fortune 500 organisations, we bring an insider's precision to career architecture. Drawing on peer-reviewed research in organisational psychology, behavioural science and leadership development, we engineer bespoke, multi-horizon career architectures, precisely tailored to each client's profile, ambition and time horizon.

We are not motivational coaches. We are end-to-end career strategists. Every engagement combines evidence-based diagnostics, strategic positioning, executive presence development and deliberate visibility-building into a single, cohesive journey, from first session to career milestone and every horizon in between. Our depth in C-Suite talent valuation means we know exactly what boards and search committees look for, and we engineer our clients to meet and exceed those criteria.

Our clients include high-potentials identified in corporate talent valuation processes, fast-track executives at Fortune 500 and Russell 1000 firms, and senior leaders transitioning into the C-Suite. What unites them is the decision to treat their career as a strategic asset, with rigour, precision and a trusted partner by their side.

Methodology

Organisational Psychology

02

Transformational Leadership

Bass & Avolio, Kotter, Lencioni and Drucker — high-performance leadership models that build lasting careers.

Scientific Communication

Performance & Productivity

Cal Newport (Deep Work), Csikszentmihalyi (Flow), BJ Fogg (Behavioral Change) — work with greater impact, not more hours.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is The Executive Edge designed for?

Our programs are designed for managers, team leaders and high-potentials at Fortune 500 and Russell 1000 firms who are targeting Head, Director or VP roles. If you are ambitious, results-driven and ready to treat your career as a strategic asset, we are the right partner for you.

What makes your approach different from traditional coaching?

We are not motivational coaches. Every program is grounded in peer-reviewed research — organisational psychology, behavioural science and leadership development. We build personalised, multi-horizon career architectures backed by analytical frameworks such as 360° diagnostics, Personal Career OKRs and Visibility KPIs. The result is a measurable strategy, not a feeling.

How long does a typical program last?

Our flagship Career Acceleration program runs 8–12 weeks with weekly 1:1 sessions. The Executive Presence Upgrade is an intensive 4-week engagement. Corporate Workshops are tailored to each organisation's timeline and objectives. All programs begin with a free 30-minute Discovery Call to determine the right fit.

Do you work online or in person?

All 1:1 programs are delivered online via video call, making them available to clients worldwide. Corporate Workshops can be delivered in-company (Lisbon and major European cities) or virtually. Our online delivery has no impact on results — our clients span four continents.

What results can I realistically expect?

87% of our clients achieve a promotion or significant role change within 12 months. Beyond title, clients report measurable improvements in executive visibility, stakeholder influence, LinkedIn authority and compensation. All results are tracked through agreed Career KPIs from the start of each engagement.

This figure reflects outcomes across our core 1:1 programs (Career Acceleration, Executive Presence Upgrade, Focus Acceleration) over a rolling 36-month dataset. "Promotion" is defined as a formal title change with expanded scope or reporting line; "significant role change" includes lateral moves into strategic roles, board appointments, or executive transitions. Data is captured via self-reported outcome tracking at 12-month follow-up, with verification through LinkedIn profile updates and client-authorized reference conversations with their HR or board contacts. Sample includes both employed executives and those in transition; results exclude clients who disengaged before month 6. Career KPIs (compensation, visibility metrics, stakeholder feedback) are documented in client contracts and tracked quarterly throughout the engagement and 12 months post-program.

What is the investment for your programs?

The Executive Presence Upgrade starts from €1,500. The Career Acceleration program starts from €2,500. Corporate Workshop pricing is provided via a custom proposal. All programs are positioned as premium, high-ROI engagements — clients consistently report compensation increases that far exceed the program investment.
